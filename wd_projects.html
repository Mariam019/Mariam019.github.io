    Educational App for Modern Irrigation (Concept & Design)

  • Description:

    • I designed an app concept aimed at educating farmers on modern irrigation techniques, focusing on improving agricultural practices and water efficiency. The app includes interactive tutorials that guide users through the installation and use of various modern irrigation systems. It also features a community space where farmers can engage in discussions, share experiences, and learn from one another’s successes and challenges. Additionally, the app integrates a marketplace that allows users to purchase irrigation equipment, find technicians, and access resources that can help improve their farming operations. The goal of the app is to empower farmers with the knowledge and tools necessary to enhance productivity, conserve water, and adopt more sustainable farming practices.

  • Technologies Used:

    • UI/UX Design, Wireframing, Miro.

    Heart monitor

  • Description:

    • I developed and designed an innovative solution to monitor users' heart rate during sports activities, ensuring real-time tracking for improved performance and safety. The project involved implementing backend code on Arduino to collect and process heart rate data efficiently. To enhance usability, I integrated the solution into a private website, allowing users to visualize and track their heart rate data over time. This system provides real-time monitoring, enabling users to gain insights into their physical activity and make informed decisions for better health and fitness management.

  • Technologies Used:

    • C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NODEMCU ESP8266, heart rate sensor XD-58C.
